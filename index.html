CanScan Support

Welcome to CanScan Support

CanScan helps you make smart shopping decisions by scanning barcodes to check product details, ingredients, allergens, Nutri-Score, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • 1. How do I scan a barcode?
    Open CanScan, point your camera at the barcode, and wait for the product details to appear.
  • 2. Where does the product data come from?
    We use the OpenFoodFacts database to provide up-to-date product information.
  • 3. What allergens does CanScan detect?
    We scan for major allergens, including gluten, milk, soybeans, eggs, nuts, fish, mustard, sesame seeds, crustaceans, sulphur dioxide, celery, molluscs, lupin, and peanuts.
  • 4. What if a product is missing or incorrect?
    You can contribute to OpenFoodFacts by updating the product details directly on their platform.
  • 5. Does CanScan work offline?
    No, an internet connection is required to fetch the latest product data.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13 Feb 2025

CanScan does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app processes barcode scans locally on your device to display product information, and no data is sent to our servers.

We use the OpenFoodFacts API to fetch product details, but no user data is collected or stored. You can read OpenFoodFacts' Privacy Policy here.

CanScan does not use advertisements, tracking, or analytics tools.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us.
